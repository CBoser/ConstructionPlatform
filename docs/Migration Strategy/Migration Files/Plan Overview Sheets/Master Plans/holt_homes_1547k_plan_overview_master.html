Framing & Foundation Notes

  • Floor sheathing: Main 1‑1/8" T&G; Upper 7/8" T&G over 14" OWSJ @ 24" o.c.
  • Sheathing: Wall/roof nailing per shear wall schedule; edge/field nailing as tagged.
  • Exterior Walls: 2×6 @ 16" o.c.; insulation per energy path.
  • Footings: Bear on undisturbed soils (1,500 psf). Reinforcing and sizes per structural schedules.
  • Garage Slab: 4" thick; slope to overhead door; thicken at bearing as required.
  • Foundation Vents: Provide 1/150 under‑floor ventilation; place a vent within 3' of each corner.
  • Radon: Provide passive radon system where required by jurisdiction.
  • Special Conditions: 2‑hour shaftliner demising walls; portal frames at garage openings where shown.

Hold‑Downs & Shear Walls

  • Shear Wall Types: SW0–SW6 per structural schedules and notes.
  • Hold‑downs: HDU4/HDU5; strap MSTC/MSTI where noted; see S‑details.
  • Anchor Bolts: 5/8" Ø typical in SDC D; plate washers 3"×3"×0.229"; spacing per schedule.
  • Sill Plates: PT sill on sill sealer at concrete contact.
  • QA / Verification Steps: Check boundary‑member stud counts, blocking, and tight nailing spacings.

Shear‑Wall Summary

  • Schedule Location: Structural sheets (S1/S3).
  • Load Range: From ~275 plf (SW0) up to ~1370 plf (SW6) depending on nailing/blocking.
  • Special Requirements: Edge blocking and staggered nailing at tight spacings; dbl. studs at boundary elements.
  • Critical Areas: Garage portal frames; large window FTAO segments; demising wall interfaces.
