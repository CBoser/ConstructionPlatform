Framing & Foundation Notes

  • Subfloor: 3/4" T&G OSB; glue and nail – 8d @ 6" edges / 10" field.
  • Sheathing: Per shear schedule; OSB/Plywood with 8d @ 6"/12" (unless noted otherwise).
  • Footings: Continuous footings per schedule; anchor bolts and plate washers per shear notes.
  • Garage Slab: 4" thick slab; compacted granular fill below; slope toward overhead door.
  • Exterior Walls: 2x6 studs with R-21 batt insulation.
  • Foundation Vents: Provide 1/150 ventilation; locate a vent within 3' of each building corner (typ. 16"x6" vents, 72 in² NFA).
  • Radon: Radon control per architectural details.
  • Special Conditions: 2-hour shaftliner demising walls; portal frames at garage openings where shown.

Hold-Downs & Shear Walls

  • Shear Wall Types: SW0–SW6 (see structural sheets).
  • Holdowns: Typical HDU4/5 at main; MSTC40 strap at upper where noted.
  • Anchor Bolts: Seismic D min. 5/8" Ø; spacing per schedule; 3"×3"×0.229" plate washers.
  • Sill Plates: PT sill on sill sealer.
  • QA/Verification Steps: Confirm edge nailing, blocking, and plate splice details at each SW type.

Shear-Wall Schedule

  • Schedule Location: S1.0 (notes & schedules), with callouts on S3.0/S5.0.
  • Load Range: Approx. 275 plf (SW0) to 1370 plf (SW6), depending on nailing and blocking.
  • Special Requirements: Panel edge blocking and staggered nailing at tight spacings; dbl. studs at boundary members.
  • Critical Areas: Portal frames at garage doors; interior FTAO segments at wide window groups.
