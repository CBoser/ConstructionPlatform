Framing & Foundation Notes

  • Floor sheathing: Main 1‑1/8" T&G; Upper 7/8" T&G over 14" OWSJ @ 24" o.c.
  • Sheathing: Walls 1/2" (typ.); roof sheathing per A6/S4.
  • Exterior Walls: 2x studs @ 16" o.c. (typ.); energy code insulation per ORSC.
  • Footings: Bearing on undisturbed native soils (1,500 psf). Reinforcing, sizes, and key details per S1/S4.
  • Garage Slab: Per S1; coordinate slopes to exterior and any thickened edges at bearing lines.
  • Foundation Vents: Under‑floor area ≈ 1,004 sf. Using 8"×14" screened vents → 10 vents required (≈0.68 sf each).
  • Radon: Provide per local jurisdiction/ORSC where required.
  • Special Conditions: Ducts sealed with mastic; balanced ventilation; continuous mechanical ventilation with automatic controls for bathrooms/laundry as noted.

Hold‑Downs & Shear Walls

  • Shear Wall Types: See S3 — Structural Shear Walls schedule and details.
  • Hold‑downs: Per S3 schedule and S4 details (specify HDU/HTT/STHD types as noted on S‑sheets during takeoff).
  • Anchor Bolts: Size/spacing/plate washers per S1.
  • Sill Plates: Per S1 (material, preservative treatment at concrete contact, fastener requirements).
  • QA / Verification Steps: Cross‑check shear wall tags to S3 values; confirm anchor bolt spacing at high‑load segments; verify any “no energy heel” roof planes against truss shop drawings.

Shear‑Wall Summary

  • Schedule Location: S3 — Structural Shear Walls.
  • Load Range: Per schedule; verify nailing, sheathing, and boundary element requirements.
  • Special Requirements: Blocking, edge nailing, hold‑down types as called out; coordinate uplift with truss reactions.
  • Critical Areas: Garage portal frames and corners; stair cores; great room openings; any segments adjacent to large glazing.
